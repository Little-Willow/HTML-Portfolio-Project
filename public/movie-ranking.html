  1. Howl's Moving Castle

    Howl's Moving Castle is a fantasy animated movie about a young woman named Sophie who is embroiled in a magical adventure between the Witch of the Waste and the Wizard Howl. Sophie must use her wits and determination to survive.

  2. Paprika

    Paprika is a sci-fi animated movie. It is about a genius psycologist and a detective working together to stop terroists from invading people's dreams and turning them into nightmares.

  3. The Four Feathers

    The Four Feather's of cowardace have been given to young Harry Faversham, a British officer who after completing his training wishes to not go to war. After receiving these feathers he determines to prove his valor and make a grand journey into the Arabian desert to return the feathers.
